Select the amount of time between the available pick up times for customers to choose from. For example, if the interval time is 10 min, pickup times will show up 10:00, 10:10 or 10:20.
Open
Close
Toggle whether customers can choose the cafe's open or closing hours to pick up their online orders. If the cafe opens at 10:00, customers can select to pick up their orders at 10:00.
